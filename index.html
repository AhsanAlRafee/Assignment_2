Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Kinder (ages 3-6) are curious and playful, learning through exploration. They develop basic skills in communication, socializing, and problem-solving.

Elementary School

Elementary school (ages 6-12) builds foundational skills in reading, writing, and math. Students develop critical thinking, teamwork, and independence.

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle school (ages 10-16) is a time of rapid growth and self-discovery. Students develop deeper academic skills, social awareness, and independence.
